Unexpected Journey 002

The Road After Bloodshed

A man in the earth, a creature of sand, and a very strange old wizard.

UJ 002 Party Level 1 Road to Vogler
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel
Joins the Road Fizban — peculiar wizard in green robes, old friend of Ispin
Location Road to Vogler; arrive at village by session's end
Session Recording
What the Night Carried
  • The dead knights' packs yielded clues about local attacks they had been investigating. The party took what they could, gave the fallen proper rest, and moved on.
  • Jasper's sleep returned him to the Dragon's Eye — the guild, the guildmaster Lucky, the deadly coin. His impossible task pressed on him like a debt: infiltrate the world of mages, find the moving Tower of Wayreth, steal the Orb of Dragonkind. None shall hide from the Dragon's Eye.
  • Kakashi, alone on watch, saw the three moons align in a way that felt deliberate and wrong. Archmage visions poured through him: fire, ice, time itself, beasts shaped by will. His hunger for greater magic stirred with new urgency.
The Man in the Earth
  • The party found a boot sticking from the road. A man half-swallowed by the earth, cheerful about it.
  • Jasper approached with caution — and was caught in the same sinking pull. A living mass of sand rose around them, with a very old man inside it and a bafflingly good attitude about the whole situation.
  • The battle was desperate. Roshi was struck hard and nearly killed. Mayriel rushed to hold her together. Kakashi's magic thundered. Jasper struck when openings appeared. The old man within unleashed a burst of his own magic — freezing the creature solid — and the party shattered it.
  • The old man introduced himself as Fizban. He joined them on the road to Vogler as though this were the most natural thing.
Fizban on the Road
  • Fizban noted that Mayriel's divine magic was remarkable. Clerics had not walked openly in centuries. If the gods were speaking again, something serious was unfolding.
  • He offered Kakashi indirect but meaningful counsel about wizardry and the paths that might await him.
  • Vogler came into view at last: festival banners, dockside activity, and the same troubling rumour from every mouth — the fish were vanishing, and strange sounds had been heard from the river.
Threads Left Open
  • Jasper's Dragon's Eye task hangs over him. The coin is still alive. Lucky expects results.
  • What is Fizban, really? Why is he here?
  • Something is in the river at Vogler.
