Unexpected Journey 001

The Night of the Eye

Five strangers. One ambush. One road made shared.

UJ 001 Party Level 1 Road to Vogler
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel
Location The road to Vogler, Solamnia
Key NPC Fallen Solamnic knights (dead on the road)
Session Recording
Before the Road Joined
  • Aratos woke from a vision of scaled warriors marching across Ansalon — a dream that felt less like sleep and more like a command. He set out for Kalaman and the Knights of Solamnia.
  • Kakashi and Jasper sat a mage trial together in a hidden chamber of invisible passages, rune-locked doors, and a false key too obvious to trust. They passed through cleverness and caution — and were rewarded with a moon-marked spellbook.
  • Roshi and Mayriel were already on the road to Vogler for the funeral of Ispin Greenshield. Strange sounds led them off the path. At the ruins of a holy place, Mayriel saw divine fire and draconic terror in vision. Roshi felt a sacred weight settle over her like a hand on the shoulder. The memory blurred after. The feeling did not.
The Ambush
  • A caravan on the road was attacked by scaled humanoid soldiers — draconic in appearance, armed, disciplined, speaking Draconic. A knight fell with an arrow in his throat.
  • Five strangers became allies in the same breath. Jasper vanished into the terrain and struck from shadow. Kakashi unleashed lightning. Roshi grappled one creature bodily and demanded answers. Mayriel heard the enemy name the Shadow Queen. Aratos recognised the creatures from his dream and charged.
  • One creature was taken alive. Under pressure, it spoke: the east had already fallen. The shadow was moving fast. Its queen would reclaim the realm. Then — rather than say more — it killed itself.
What We Carry Forward
  • The Solamnic knights were buried beside the road. The scaled attackers were burned — their bodies decayed at an unnatural rate, and no one wanted to find out what that meant.
  • First shared knowledge: Dragon Armies. The Shadow Queen. The east already gone.
  • Five strangers now share a road. None of them planned it. Vogler still waits ahead.
Threads Left Open
  • What are these creatures? How far does the Dragon Army already extend?
  • Why did the prisoner choose death over capture? What was it protecting?
  • What did the gods say to Roshi at the ruined holy site?
