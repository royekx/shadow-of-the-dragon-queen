Unexpected Journey 009

The Reliquary of Lost Oaths

A door that tests oaths. A wraith that was once a knight. And Ispin's stew at the end of it all.

UJ 009 Party Level 4 Outside Kalaman
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel · Aeimos (returns)
Key NPCs Fizban · Sir Cthondor (fallen Knight Radiant, freed from corruption) · Pale Woman (mysterious guide)
Location The wilds outside Kalaman — a hidden cave and the reliquary of the Knights Radiant below it
Session Recording
The Hunt
  • Fizban wanted to make Ispin's stew. He needed meat. The party went hunting.
  • Aeimos had returned — moving at the edges as was his way, unseen until Jasper noticed him, because of course Jasper noticed him.
  • Mayriel pursued deer and found herself separated. As Aeimos moved to point out the deer, his altered senses betrayed him — he stepped too loudly. Mayriel whirled. And behind her, Aeimos saw something else: a pale woman with pointed ears and long flowing hair, watching him with unnerving calm. She pointed toward where the others had gone. Then vanished.
  • The bear trail led to a cave. No bear inside. Only a faint blue glow from deeper within — and on the floor, purplish-black soot. The same mark left by the Ashen Vigils on the river.
The Reliquary
  • A heavy stone door bore the symbol of the Knights Radiant — the familiar sword, but with six points instead of four. Symbols lit around it. It was not asking for a key. It was asking for oaths.
  • Aratos stepped forward and felt warmth and storm rise in him. He spoke the words that had shaped him: life before death, strength before weakness, journey before destination. The word Valor lit.
  • Aeimos felt the weight of the moment and spoke beneath his breath: hopelessness is not my privilege. The word Resolve lit. He wept — not from weakness, but from the force of something ancient recognising the oath inside him.
  • Roshi and Mayriel each answered the door in turn. Foresight. Then Protection. The door opened.
  • Beyond: murals lining the passage — Knights of Solamnia and Knights Radiant standing together beneath a floating city, then those same forces diminished as the city fell burning from the sky. One figure appeared repeatedly, bearing a horn. Parts of the mural had been recently defaced with the same purplish-black ash.
The Necromancer and the Wraith
  • In the tomb chamber below: armoured dead along the walls, a central knight holding a horn, and before him — a withered necromancer chanting over a spectral black rose with two petals. Two knightly dead already walked.
  • Kakashi's Witch Bolt crashed into the necromancer. Jasper fired from shadow. Aeimos struck a risen knight down with storm-charged lightning from distance. Aratos called his ancestors — bald guardian, kingly figure, blue-hued spearman — and drove the Emberwake Greataxe into the caster. Mayriel's sword erupted in divine radiant rose-thorns.
  • A black-purple orb filled the chamber with whispering, monstrous sounds and gnawing cold. Kakashi maintained his lightning through the pain. Jasper broke the necromancer's concentration. As Aeimos finished the caster, it spoke its dying words in service to Lord Soth. The black rose surged — and Sir Cthondor rose as a wraith.
  • The wraith spread despair through the room like a physical thing. Most of the party froze. Aeimos read the situation quickly: the ritual was incomplete. The wraith was dangerous but not at full power. Radiant magic was its weakness.
  • Mayriel's sacred flame burned. Roshi turned the undead. Kakashi drove the Boilerdrake Heart past its limits — lightning and fire together. Roshi's spiritual weapon took the shape of a rose. Its petals closed around the wraith like a divine snare. The shadow was extinguished.
Sir Cthondor
  • The knight appeared as he had been — solemn, confused, grateful. He thanked the fellowship for freeing the tomb from the evil that had taken hold. He named Aratos and Aeimos children of Cathan and said they had proven themselves.
  • He raised his horn and blew. The corrupted knightly dead rose from their fallen forms and returned to rest. The chamber went still and quiet with reverence.
  • Aeimos bowed. This was the first true reliquary of the Knights Radiant he had ever known.
Claimed
  • Bone Armor — heavy with necrotic energy, taken from the necromancer's body.
  • Horn of Sir Cthondor — when blown by one of Cathan's bloodline, summons two spectral knights to serve. Left with Aratos by Aeimos before he slipped away again.
  • The party eventually found the bear. Fizban made Ispin's stew. The refugees ate. Darrett arrived covered in training sweat. For one evening, there was warmth.
Threads Left Open
  • The pale woman told Aeimos: Lord Soth seeks to corrupt all knightly legacies until his own curse is lifted. He will continue. More tombs exist. More knights are at risk.
  • Aeimos left following the pale woman's direction — his road and Aratos's are aligned but not the same.
  • Jasper found a note in Thieves' Cant, marked with the Dragon's Eye: “We need to talk.”
  • The Horn of Sir Cthondor is with Aratos. Two spectral knights, waiting to be called.
