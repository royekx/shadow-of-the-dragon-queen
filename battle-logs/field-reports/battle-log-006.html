Unexpected Journey 006

The Dragon Queen's Demand

An ultimatum at sunset. A javelin through a messenger's heart.

UJ 006 Party Level 3 Vogler
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel
Key NPCs Cudgel · Jeyev (loyal lieutenant) · Mayor Raven · Lord Bakaris · Than (gnome tinkerer)
Location Vogler — gate, cliff above, Thornwall Keep
Session Recording
The Ultimatum
  • Cudgel returned with her loyal lieutenant Jeyev. The Red Army camp to the northwest was confirmed — real, positioned, and coming.
  • A rider in black and red armor appeared at Vogler's gate. Mayor Raven received his scroll. The message from the Red Dragon Army: open the gates by sunset, quarter and feed the soldiers, and comply. Refusal would bring swift and final mercy. It was not a negotiation.
  • Roshi, from Thornwall Keep, spotted armored figures on the clifftop above the gate. The messenger had not come alone. Than the gnome tinkerer offered unstable explosives as booby traps and help repairing boats for evacuation.
  • Lord Bakaris tried to insert himself into the planning. Kakashi turned his vanity into usefulness — praising him as exactly the authority needed to carry word to Kalaman. Bakaris preferred writing a letter with his seal. Even so.
Vogler Told
  • Mayor Raven gathered her people and told them the truth: surrender by sunset, or face destruction. Panic broke out immediately.
  • Kakashi stepped forward, struck his staff, and commanded silence with a flash of power. He told them there was a plan, that the mayor and defenders were working together, and that people needed to begin packing, loading boats, and preparing to move. His force gave way to gentleness. Villagers began offering boats, labour, and aid.
Buying Time
  • The plan: get Jeyev out to warn and rally Cudgel's loyal mercenaries, while distracting both the messenger at the gate and the watchers on the cliff above.
  • The cliff plan fell apart almost immediately. Aratos tried to bluff a watcher and shove him off the edge — a blade came to Aratos's throat instead. Mayriel called down moonlit radiant power and burned one watcher to ash. She was struck hard and fell unconscious, brought back by a healing potion.
  • Below: Kakashi created the sound of someone screaming. Roshi ran toward it to sell the illusion. Jeyev rode. The mounted messenger tried to intercept — Roshi drew his attention and absorbed the blows instead.
  • Aratos lined up his javelin and threw. It struck the messenger through the heart. The horse fled. Jeyev was gone into the distance.
  • The cliffside enemies fell. Cudgel climbed into the fight near the end. The watchers were dead or ash.
Recovered
  • 2 healing potions, a Periapt of Wound Closure (preserves from death when falling), a blue-gemmed ring (protective magic), and gold.
Threads Left Open
  • The evacuation has begun. The boats are not fully ready. Sunset is hours away.
  • Jeyev is riding. Whether the mercenaries will reach Vogler in time is unknown.
  • The Red Army did not expect resistance. They will come regardless.
