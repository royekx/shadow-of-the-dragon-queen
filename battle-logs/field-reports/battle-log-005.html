Unexpected Journey 005

Shadows in the Woods

A broken army, a spy's nerve, and a hundred soldiers waiting in the dark.

UJ 005 Party Level 3 Vogler & Surrounds
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel
Split Party Scouts: Aratos, Kakashi, Jasper, Darrett · In town: Roshi, Mayriel
Key NPCs Cudgel · Mayor Raven · Darrett · Old Man Jenkins (Dragon's Eye)
Location Vogler, and the woods to the northwest
Session Recording
The Interrogation
  • The captured mercenary was woken at the Brass Crab. Cudgel nearly lost herself to rage. Mayor Raven stepped in before it became pure vengeance.
  • Aratos leaned close and made the threat quietly clear. The man broke. Cudgel's lieutenant Gragonis had gone into the woods and returned with gold. Some mercenaries were paid to betray Cudgel — slaughter the militia, leave Vogler defenseless. The coin had been good enough.
  • The traitors had retreated toward the mercenary camp. Something larger waited beyond the woods. Cudgel insisted on going herself to find who was still loyal. No escort.
Into the Woods
  • Before the party left, Aratos was struck by a vision: an impossible sky, a colossal tree standing between flame and frost. An ancient, stern figure offered a choice — blades bound in fury and vengeance, or an axe of cold restraint and resolve. “Choose, boy. The gods are watching.”
  • Jasper moved ahead alone, vanishing into the dark as only he could. What he found was not a handful of mercenaries but one to two hundred armed soldiers — organized, supplied, and positioned. Not raiders. Soldiers waiting.
  • A patrol with a draconian officer drew dangerously close. Jasper used his Dragon's Eye coin to disguise himself as a guard, pretended to relieve himself against a tree, and bluffed his way through. Aratos, watching from distance, caught sight of the draconian shape in the dark.
  • Kakashi sent his raven to follow Cudgel at a distance, unseen.
In Town
  • Roshi laid hands on a wounded fisherman. He asked whether she had been touched by the gods. Neither of them answered — because both knew what it meant. Clerics had not walked openly in centuries.
  • Mayriel found a woman hiding a fatal wound while insisting others needed care first. Golden divine light healed her. She wept. The question surfaced again: had the gods returned?
  • Jenkins — Old Man Jenkins, Dragon's Eye — was packing to flee. He had no intention of dying for Vogler. Jasper's decision to stay surprised him. When Jasper said the town was good and its people needed help, Jenkins gave him something before vanishing: two healing potions and special bolts designed to leech life from their targets.
Threads Left Open
  • The Red Army camp to Vogler's northwest is real — 100 to 200 soldiers, organized and waiting.
  • Aratos's divine vision: a choice not yet fully made. The gods are watching.
  • Jasper's Dragon's Eye coin is more than a disguise — it is also a leash.
  • Cudgel has not yet returned.
