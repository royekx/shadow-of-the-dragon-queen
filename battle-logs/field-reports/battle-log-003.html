Unexpected Journey 003

The Beast Beneath Crustdurn Isle

Vogler's warmth, a haunted island, and the thing in the cave beneath it.

UJ 003 Party Level 2 Vogler
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel
Joins the Road Aeimos — Aratos's cousin, Stormblast markings, returns a stolen ring as a greeting
Key NPCs Becklin Uth Viharin (retired Knight of Solamnia) · Fizban · Aeimos
Location Vogler — the Brass Crab, Gertrude's smithy, the docks, Crustdurn Isle
Session Recording
Vogler's Welcome
  • The Brass Crab offered food, warmth, and Ispin Greenshield stories — tales of goblins, owlbears, mountains, and narrow escapes, each one half legend and half truth. Roshi ordered crab ramen despite the obvious danger to her health.
  • Aratos met Becklin Uth Viharin at Gertrude's smithy — a retired Knight of Solamnia, grounded and direct. He told her of the vision that had set him on the road. She did not dismiss him. She listened. That was enough.
  • At the docks, Mayriel touched the fish statue and was struck by a vision: reptilian soldiers marching beneath the three moons, a horned woman commanding slaughter, a five-headed dragon breathing fire in five colours — and then platinum fire rising to shield Mayriel from annihilation. When the vision ended, Fizban found her shaken and told her to listen when the gods spoke.
  • Aeimos appeared — same Stormblast markings as Aratos, and the audacity to have already stolen his cousin's ring as a greeting. Aratos told him of the vision and the danger ahead. Aeimos agreed to come toward Kalaman.
Crustdurn Isle
  • The strange sounds from the river, the vanishing fish, and a lonely island locals called haunted all pointed the same direction. Kakashi's raven scouted an abandoned shack on the isle. The party rented a rowboat and crossed.
  • Inside the shack: a hatch. Below the hatch: a cavern of fish bones, damp stone, and old writings suggesting something down here had once needed to be fed.
  • The creature that rose was monstrous — frog-like, tentacled, and hungry. It swallowed Roshi whole mid-battle.
  • Aratos drove lightning through its body. Jasper found killing angles. The Stormblast cousins pressed together and brought it down. Roshi cut her way free.
Recovered
  • From the creature's hoard: a moon-touched sword and boots marked by strange magic.
  • Aeimos cut out the creature's tongue as a trophy. No one asked why.
  • Vogler had lived beside a waking threat for years without knowing. For now, at least, the island was quiet. Becklin received their report with surprised gratitude.
Threads Left Open
  • What do Mayriel's dock visions mean? The horned woman — who is she?
  • The vision's five-headed dragon and platinum fire — this feels like more than prophecy.
  • Fizban knows more than he lets on. He watches Mayriel closely.
