Unexpected Journey 004

Tales, Tears, and Treachery

Ispin goes into the river. The mock battle turns real. Vogler will never be the same.

UJ 004 Party Level 2 Vogler
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel · Aeimos
Key NPCs Becklin · Cudgel Ironsmile · Darrett (squire) · Bakaris the Younger · Fizban
Location Vogler — Vingaard River (funeral), the Brass Crab, Thornwall Keep, High Hill
Session Recording
The Funeral
  • At sunset, Ispin Greenshield's body was placed in a small boat on the Vingaard River. Becklin and Cudgel lowered it. The people watched until it was gone.
  • At the Brass Crab: grief became laughter, tankards clashed, and Ispin stories rose from every corner. Fizban told one about a stolen pie and an offended prince. Roshi told of Ispin pursuing honey in a bear den and waiting for the bears to wake so he could compliment them on it. Roshi had to wrestle the mother bear to save him.
  • Kakashi spoke more quietly. He described Ispin as a man guided by curiosity rather than fear — someone who treated life as an invitation to explore, ask questions, change, and find joy in the unknown. The room went still. Then raised their drinks.
  • Bakaris the Younger interrupted to mock the crowd's affection for a dead common adventurer. Becklin told him if he had nothing fond to say, he should leave for the evening. He left in a huff. He did not improve.
Morning Gifts
  • At Thornwall Keep, Becklin gave Roshi Ispin's Green Shield. When Roshi took it, it felt like the shield had come home.
  • Fizban left for Kalaman at first light, taking Aeimos with him. Before departing, he left Kakashi a spell.
  • Mayriel woke to a golden finch and a pendant to aid her divine path.
  • Becklin's squire Darrett Highwater brought the party to Thornwall Keep for the morning ceremony.
High Hill
  • The Kingfisher Festival's centrepiece: a mock battle reenacting Vogler's founding. Mayor Raven led the parade. The party took part. It was pageantry — until an unpadded blade opened a real wound and a villager screamed.
  • The mock battle became a massacre. Cudgel's mercenaries had been paid to turn. Jasper killed a traitor before Darrett fell. Aratos held the line. Roshi stood with Ispin's shield and moon-touched steel. Mayriel's divine light burned wild and devastating. Kakashi pressed magic into the fight from the air with the winged boots.
  • A half-ogre drove Roshi to near-death. She answered with divine fire. Her blade erupted with searing power and she refused to give ground. Jasper put an arrow through the ogre's head as it fell.
  • Becklin and Cudgel arrived and pushed the remainder into retreat. On the ogre: a note. "For your services from the Red Army."
Threads Left Open
  • Cudgel's lieutenant Gragonis was paid by the Red Army. Where is he?
  • The Red Army paid mercenaries to leave Vogler vulnerable. Vulnerable for what?
  • Bakaris the Younger — useless, or hiding something?
  • Jasper's secret meeting with Old Man Jenkins: Dragon's Eye, Lucky, the Orb of Dragonkind. Jenkins also sold him the winged boots using the party's shared gold. Jasper called it a "slick deal."
