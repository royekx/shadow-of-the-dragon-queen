Unexpected Journey 007

Flames Over Vogler

The dragon was a machine. The loss was real.

UJ 007 Party Level 3 Vogler — The Docks
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel · Winkle Dust (gnome, joins mid-session)
Lost This Session Becklin Uth Viharin — falls defending the dock retreat
Key Enemies Theros Flametongue (Red Army commander) · The Boilerdrake (mechanical dragon)
Location Vogler docks · Vingaard River (escape)
Session Recording
The Dragon Comes
  • Evacuation nearly complete. The last boats strained at the docks. Then wings in the smoke, and someone cried the word not spoken with certainty in Krynn for centuries: dragon.
  • The creature swept over the docks and breathed fire. Those still in its path were reduced to ash. This was not rumour. Not a vision. Not old legend remembered wrong. A dragon had come to Vogler.
  • Winkle Dust launched himself from the gnome-flingerCatapult at Thornwall Keep, sailed through the air, and landed on his feet at the docks. He noticed what others didn't: the dragon was not alone — a humanoid rider had ridden it and leapt down.
  • Mayriel raised her holy symbol and wrapped the party in dim twilight radiance — a sphere of calm amid burning streets. In that small light, they found enough courage to stand.
Theros Flametongue
  • The rider landed — green-skinned, orcish, carrying a flaming axe. Theros Flametongue mocked what he had heard of Vogler's heroes and met Aratos like a man who wanted a worthy kill. Ancestral silhouettes gathered around Aratos as he charged — large guardians, hooded figures, silent watchers.
  • Jasper rose on the winged boots, forced his fear into focus under the impossible sight of the dragon, and put arrows into it. Kakashi surged forward with lightning screaming like a thousand birds and struck Theros with crackling force.
  • Jasper looked more carefully at the dragon — and understood. It did not move like flesh. Mechanisms, smoke, seams, figures visible within its frame. The dragon was a machine, crewed by soldiers inside. Arrows and spells shifted to seek seams and weak points.
  • Mayriel killed Theros. Necrotic force drove him to one knee. Her spiritual sword took his throat. He snarled that he would not die to nothings. He died anyway. Purple blood spilled across the dock.
The Machine Falls
  • Aratos heard the cold voice again — the one that had been testing him — and hurled his javelin into the sky. Lightning tore through the construct's core. The Boilerdrake's mechanisms failed. The great machine crashed into the docks in a storm of fire, metal, and ruin.
  • Roshi went down, preserved from death only by the magic she carried.
  • One operator survived in the wreckage. Mayriel saw him. Jasper fired but could not finish him. Kakashi walked into the burning remains, reached in, and released another surge of lightning. The operator died screaming — and his death triggered an explosion that dropped Kakashi too. Memories flashed through him as he fell: a village burning, people crying, a past he had tried to bury beneath distance.
  • Mayriel pledged her family signet ring to Paladine — a promise of obedience when the god would one day ask something of her. The ring glowed with holy light. The golden canary swept in. Radiance washed over the party and restored them just enough to move.
The Fall of Vogler
  • From the boat, the party watched Becklin Uth Viharin and Cudgel Ironsmile still fighting at the waterfront — holding back a tall woman with charred skin, blackened armor, and a massive sword.
  • Cudgel was kicked through a wall. Becklin fought on. She reached the edge of the dock. She looked out toward the departing boat. She raised her hands as if to say: it is all right.
  • Roshi loosed an arrow. The distance was too great.
  • Blades struck Becklin from behind. She fell into the dark water. Vogler disappeared behind smoke, fire, and ash.
  • As the boat moved, the dead Theros Flametongue rose from his body on the burning dock, touched by flame. A voice called after the fleeing survivors: “Run as far as you can. You will not escape the flames of the Dragon Queen.”
Claimed from the Ruins
  • Emberwake Greataxe — Theros's axe, still warm. Taken by Aratos.
  • Boilerdrake Heart — a hot glowing gem from the dragon-machine's core, the source of its fire. Taken by Kakashi.
Threads Left Open
  • Vogler is gone. Becklin is gone.
  • The charred woman with the blackened sword — who is she, and who does she serve?
  • Theros rose from death on the burning dock. How?
  • Kakashi's burning memory — what village? What happened?
