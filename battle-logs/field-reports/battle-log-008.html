Unexpected Journey 008

Ashes on the River

The dead rise from the water. Kalaman opens its gates — at a price.

UJ 008 Party Level 4 Vingaard River · Kalaman
The Embers Aratos · Kakashi · Jasper · Roshi · Mayriel · Than Rookledust · Darrett (ally)
Key NPCs Mayor Raven · Darrett Highwater · Lord Bakaris · Governor Miat · Marshal Vendri
Location Vingaard River (the journey) · Kalaman (arrival, council chamber)
Session Recording

No recording available for this session.

The River
  • The boats moved downstream with the survivors of Vogler. Smoke marked the horizon behind them. Darrett sat shaken — Becklin gone, the town burning, and the weight of survival pressing on him. Roshi brought him Becklin's armor. He could not take it. Not yet.
  • Kakashi attuned to the Boilerdrake Heart. Aratos took up the Emberwake Greataxe. The river moved and the silence held for a short while.
  • Then the dead appeared in the water. Hands reached over the sides of boats. Villagers who tried to help were pulled beneath. The river became a mirror for grief — showing each person something sharp enough to draw them close.
  • The visions: Darrett saw Becklin and was dragged under. Jasper saw Lucky, who warned time on the Dragon's Eye task was running out. Aratos saw two knights — Cathan Twice-Born and Lord Soth — legacy and its corruption, standing as opposite warnings. Mayriel saw the Dragon Queen, who promised her sword would find her. Roshi saw Ispin, who asked whether she had truly protected his people. She answered truthfully. It kept her from the water.
  • The apparitions rose as Ashen Vigils — burning with violet and purple flame. The party fought them back and pulled the taken from the river. Somewhere beyond mortal sight, Lord Soth's awareness brushed against their path and lingered.
Kalaman
  • Mayor Raven composed herself for the sake of her people. She asked the party to speak on Vogler's behalf before the council. Darrett donned Becklin's armor — not as a claim of worth already proven, but as a promise to grow into it.
  • At the gate: Bakaris the Younger was already inside. He'd reached the council before the refugees. He called the displaced villagers "fishfolk."
  • In the chamber: Governor Miat, Marshal Vendri, Lord Bakaris, Darrett, and the party. Bakaris tried to twist the proceedings. Aratos silenced him physically. The party offered their service in Kalaman's military efforts in exchange for aid and shelter for Vogler's people.
  • Darrett stepped forward and agreed to serve as intermediary — keeping the party from full military conscription while honouring the promise made for Vogler. Aratos identified himself as a Knight Radiant. Marshal Vendri acknowledged the title. The council granted the refugees' refuge.
  • Jasper, listening where others could not, overheard the council's private discussion. He also noticed one council figure shimmer faintly with illusion — the mark of the Dragon's Eye. The guild had eyes inside Kalaman's government.
After the Council
  • Kakashi and Jasper visited Wyhan's Apothecary with their High Sorcery scrolls. Wyhan determined both would test for the White Robes. A practical exam was scheduled several weeks out.
  • Sunscale — the golden finch that had appeared to Mayriel — returned to Fizban, then made its own choice: it stayed with Mayriel. The bird is not what it appears to be. Fizban sensed something profound in the moment of its choosing.
  • The party returned to Mayor Raven and the refugees. Vogler's people had walls above them and food on the way. The road from Vogler had ended. The war had not.
Threads Left Open
  • The Dragon's Eye has an operative inside Kalaman's council chamber. Jasper knows.
  • Aratos must prove himself as a Knight Radiant. Marshal Vendri is watching.
  • Sunscale chose Mayriel. A small golden bird with a mystery walking with the fellowship.
  • The Ashen Vigils and the shadow of Lord Soth — his attention has grazed their path.
  • High Sorcery tests for Kakashi and Jasper are coming.
