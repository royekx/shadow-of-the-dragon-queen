Aeimos is Aratos's cousin, carrying the same storm-marked blood and the same capacity for dangerous power. Where Aratos burns with vision and fury, Aeimos moves with discipline and precision. A martial artist trained in the way of the monks, he joined The Embers not out of summons, but out of kinship.

He is quiet by nature, but his silence is the quiet of a storm gathering its strength. Aeimos has fought alongside his cousin, faced the same shadows, and proven that sometimes the greatest power comes not from anger, but from mastery over oneself.
