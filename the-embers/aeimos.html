Aeimos Stormblessed

Discipline walks in silence

Class Monk — Storm-charged
Affiliation Knights Radiant · The Embers (irregular)
Rank Unattached — field operative
Equipment Storm-charged gauntlets · Pipe (altered senses)
Status Active — field (location unknown)

Aeimos Stormblessed arrived in Vogler by stealing his cousin's ring and presenting this as a normal greeting. It was, for Aeimos. He operates at the edges of groups — appearing when something needs to be done, disappearing before anyone can ask follow-up questions. He has a pipe whose smoke alters his senses and a habit of noticing things at the precise moment that noticing them matters.

He carries the same Stormblast lineage as Aratos, and the same weight of the name Cathan. In the reliquary beneath the hills outside Kalaman, Sir Cthondor named them both children of Cathan and said they had proven themselves. Aeimos understood the significance before the words had fully settled, and bowed to the knight's ghost with a sincerity that was not performed.

A pale woman with pointed ears and long flowing hair appears to him — not often, and never for long. She has pointed him toward danger and away from it in equal measure. She told him what Lord Soth is doing: corrupting knightly legacies one by one, defacing reliquaries, twisting the honored dead, until his own curse is lifted or every memory of honour is gone. Aeimos has decided this is his problem to deal with, which is a large decision to make quietly before walking back into the dark.

He left the Horn of Sir Cthondor with Aratos before he left. He did not explain why he couldn't keep it himself. He didn't need to. Aratos is the one with roots right now. Aeimos moves better without them.
