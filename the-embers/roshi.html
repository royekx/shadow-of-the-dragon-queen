Roshi

Humble strength in humble hands

Class Paladin of Kiri-Jolith
Affiliation The Embers · Kiri-Jolith (blessed)
Rank Soldier — Kalaman
Equipment Ispin's Green Shield · Moon-touched sword
Status Active

Roshi was traveling to Vogler for a funeral when she heard something calling from off the road — not words, but a summons. She found the ruins of a holy place and stood in the dark, not afraid, and something met her there. Kiri-Jolith answered. When she left those ruins, she was no longer simply herself. She had become the god's instrument, blessed to stand between others and ruin.

She carries Ispin's Green Shield, a gift from Becklin Uth Viharin given the morning the city burned. Becklin said it belonged with someone still willing to stand in front of danger. When Roshi took it, she understood immediately — the shield had found its bearer. She fights with it as though Ispin stands beside her still, which she believes is the truth. A paladin's protection is not lonely. Kiri-Jolith does not shield alone.

Her divine gifts manifest as warmth through the hands, golden light that turns back shadow, and a spiritual weapon that takes the form of a rose. She does not summon them. They arrive when the moment calls for mercy, precision, or courage. At the reliquary of Sir Cthondor, when a wraith rose to claim corrupted oaths, the rose closed around it like a thornèd binding, and the shadow was released back to peace. People who receive her protection ask if she has been chosen by Kiri-Jolith. She tells them: yes. The god does not hide.

She honored Ispin at the Brass Crab on the night of his funeral — told of honey, a bear den, a bear he wanted to thank, and Roshi wrestling the mother bear to save him from his own reckless kindness. The room laughed. She meant it as a paladin's tribute: to show what a life of protection looks like, even when it costs you. That was the right kind of story to tell.
