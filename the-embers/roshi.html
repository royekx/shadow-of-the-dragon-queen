Roshi walked the roads toward Vogler to attend a funeral and say goodbye to an old name — the name of an impossible hero. Instead, she found something larger: a calling, a purpose, and companions who would come to matter more than anything.

She is a warrior devoted to the protection of those who cannot protect themselves. Humble in speech but fierce in action, Roshi has learned that honor is not a thing spoken of — it is something lived, moment by moment, choice by choice. She carries a shield that once belonged to a legend, and she has earned the right to bear it.
