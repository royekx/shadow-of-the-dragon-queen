Roshi

Cleric. Bearer of Ispin's Shield. The one the gods chose to touch.

Class Cleric
Affiliation The Embers
Rank Soldier — Kalaman
Equipment Ispin's Green Shield · Moon-touched sword
Status Active

Roshi was traveling to Vogler for a funeral when strange sounds pulled her off the road. The ruins of an old holy place waited in the dark. Something was already there, and when it settled into her — like a hand upon the shoulder — she did not resist it. She has not been the same since, and she would say that is exactly right.

She carries Ispin's Green Shield, a gift from Becklin Uth Viharin given the morning of the day Vogler burned. Becklin said it belonged with someone still willing to stand in front of danger. When Roshi took it, it felt like the shield had come home to something. She fights with it as though Ispin is still there in the weight of it, which she thinks is probably true in some way she doesn't need to explain.

Her divine gifts manifest as warmth through the hands, golden light, and a spiritual weapon that takes the form of a rose. She does not summon the rose deliberately. It arrives when the situation calls for precision. At the reliquary of Sir Cthondor, it closed around a wraith like a snare, and the shadow was extinguished. People who receive her healing ask if she has been touched by the gods. She gives them the honest answer: she thinks she has.

She told Ispin's story at the Brass Crab on the night of his funeral — honey, a bear den, a bear he wanted to thank, and Roshi wrestling the mother bear to save him from his own cheerful recklessness. The room laughed. She meant it as tribute. It was the right kind.

Threads & Open Questions
  • A divine calling settled into her at the holy site on the road to Vogler. Its nature and origin remain unclear. The gods have not spoken in words — only in weight.
  • Ispin's Green Shield is in her hands. The shield feels purposeful. What that purpose asks of her is still forming.
  • Her spiritual weapon takes the form of a rose. This is not random. It means something about the divine she carries.
  • In the river vision, she saw Ispin ask whether she had truly protected his people. She answered that she had done what she could. That was enough to hold against the current.
