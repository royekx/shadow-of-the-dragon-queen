Mayriel is a healer and warrior who hears the voice of Paladine in moments of darkness and divine clarity. She came to the roads seeking to understand what the gods were saying — and she found her answer in fire and purpose.

She has stood between her companions and death. She has called down divine wrath upon those who would harm the innocent. And she has carried the weight of being the light in a world growing darker. Mayriel's faith is not blind — it is sharp, tested, and earned through sacrifice.
