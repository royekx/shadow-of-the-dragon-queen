Mayriel

Faith made flesh and shield

Class Cleric of Paladine
Affiliation The Embers · Paladine (pledged)
Rank Soldier — Kalaman
Equipment Sunscale · Divine pendant · Family signet ring — pledged
Status Active — pledge outstanding

Mayriel was at Vogler's docks, one hand on a fish statue touched by locals for luck, when the vision arrived without invitation. A horned woman commanding slaughter beneath the three moons. A five-headed dragon breathing fire in five colours. And then — before annihilation — platinum fire rising to shield her from it. She did not choose to see it. When it ended, Fizban found her shaken, and told her that if the gods were speaking, she should listen.

She is a cleric of Paladine at a moment when that should be impossible. The gods left Krynn generations ago. Clerics have not walked openly in centuries. Mayriel's divine power is not a theological position — it is something that happens when she reaches for it, which makes it stranger to witness than simple faith, and more difficult to argue with. People ask her if she has been touched by the gods. The honest answer frightens her slightly less than it used to.

At Vogler's burning docks, she pledged her family signet ring to Paladine — a promise of obedience at a moment yet to come, terms unspecified. The ring glowed with holy light when she offered it. Sunscale, the golden canary that had followed Fizban, witnessed this and then chose to remain with her. Sunscale is not simply a bird. The truth of what it is shimmers at the edges of ordinary perception, visible only in the right light, which is the light Mayriel increasingly sees by.

On the Vingaard River, the Dragon Queen appeared to her in vision and promised that her sword would find her. Mayriel heard it. She has not forgotten it, and she has decided that the correct response is to be somewhere else when the sword arrives.
