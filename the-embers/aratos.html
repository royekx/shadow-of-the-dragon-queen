Aratos carries the mark of something larger than himself — a vision that came to him like a summons, showing him a world conquered by dragons and their armies. He woke from that dream with thunder in his veins and a purpose carved into his bones: to reach Kalaman, to find the Knights of Solamnia, and to fight.

He is driven by that vision, and by a bond of blood with Aeimos, his cousin. Aratos leads not always by choice, but by the weight of what he carries. He has faced the fears of a fallen town, carried refugees to safety, and stood against the Shadow Queen's servants. The storm in him grows stronger with each battle.
