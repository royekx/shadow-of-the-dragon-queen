Aratos Stormblessed

Driven by thunder and conviction

Class Barbarian — Ancestral Guardian
Affiliation Knights Radiant · The Embers
Rank Soldier — Kalaman
Equipment Emberwake Greataxe · Horn of Sir Cthondor
Status Active

The dream came on the Night of the Eye — scaled warriors marching in ordered ranks, a divine command settling into the bones before consciousness caught up. When Aratos Stormblessed woke, the vision did not fade. It pointed him toward Kalaman and the Knights of Solamnia, and he followed it the same day, without waiting to understand what had chosen him or why.

He carries the Stormblast name — a lineage that makes itself known in battle. When the rage rises, ancestral silhouettes gather around him: a towering bald guardian, a kingly figure in a hood, a blue-hued warrior with a spear at rest. They do not speak. They stand, and something in the enemy takes notice of them too.

A Knight Radiant by title and intention, Aratos fights with the kind of certainty that reads as recklessness from the outside. He was told the gods are watching. He acts as if they are, even when the only evidence is the weight of a javelin leaving his hand at exactly the right moment.

In the reliquary beneath the hills outside Kalaman, Sir Cthondor named him a child of Cathan and said he had proven himself. Aratos is not sure what that means yet. He intends to find out before it matters in a way he is not ready for.
