A scholar and student of magic, Kakashi is driven by curiosity and a need to understand the world around him — especially the nature of magic itself. He carries a familiar, a clever raven, and questions that most mages dare not ask.

He joined The Embers not seeking adventure, but because the road they walked demanded his skills. He has studied ancient magic, faced the impossible, and proven that wit and knowledge are weapons as sharp as any blade. Kakashi sees the world through the lens of understanding, and he is determined to survive long enough to learn its deepest secrets.
