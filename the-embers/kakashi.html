Kakashi

Curious mind, cautious heart

Class Wizard — White Robes
Affiliation Mages of High Sorcery · The Embers
Rank Soldier — Kalaman
Equipment Boilerdrake Heart · Moon-marked spellbook · Raven familiar
Status Active — High Sorcery test pending

Kakashi passed the mage trial with focus and discipline — invisible passages, rune-locked doors, a false key too obvious to trust. The moon-marked spellbook that followed him out of the testing chamber was only the beginning. Krynn's changing sky keeps offering him things, and his hunger for what it offers has not learned patience.

He watches the moons the way a general reads terrain. On watch above the road to Vogler, he saw them align in a way that felt deliberate and wrong — visions of archmages shaping fire, ice, beasts, and time itself, and a hunger for greater magic that has not let go of him since. He did not choose ambition. He simply noticed how far his reach falls short of what he can see.

He is capable of gentleness in moments that matter. At Vogler, when panic broke through the crowd, Kakashi struck his staff and commanded silence with a flash of power — then let his force give way to steadiness, told the people there was a plan, and meant it. The crowd moved. That kind of thing stays with people.

Something in his past burns. When the mechanical dragon fell at Vogler's docks and the final explosion dropped him, memories surfaced unbidden — a village on fire, people crying — and he fell into them rather than through them. He has not spoken of it. The raven knows, and does not say.
