Jasper hides sharp steel beneath quick wit and a mask of easy charm. A thief by trade, a trickster by nature, he joined The Embers for reasons he has never fully explained. What is clear is that he will not abandon them now — not when the world itself is at stake.

His fingers move faster than most can see. His mind works in angles and distances others miss. He has saved his companions with cunning, carried them through impossible places, and proven that sometimes the smallest person in the room is the most dangerous. Jasper is a shadow that chooses to stand in the light.
