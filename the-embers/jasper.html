Jasper Quickwit

Rogue. Dragon's Eye operative. Present wherever no one expected him.

Class Rogue
Affiliation Dragon's Eye (bound) · The Embers
Rank Soldier — Kalaman
Equipment Winged boots · Dragon's Eye coin · Leeching bolts
Status Active — Dragon's Eye task outstanding

Jasper Quickwit passed the mage trial with caution and cleverness — invisible passages, a locked door, a false key that was obviously wrong. He saw through the false promise before anyone else did, and he has been doing a version of that ever since. He is good at reading what a situation wants him to believe, and very good at not believing it.

He is not really here for the magic. He is here because the Dragon's Eye guild bound him with a coin — a small, deadly thing that carries consequences — and gave him a task framed as a mission: find the Tower of Wayreth, which moves and does not want to be found, infiltrate it, and steal the Orb of Dragonkind from within. The guildmaster Lucky Leppomanto was clear that failure is not a negotiation. None shall hide from the Dragon's Eye.

Jasper is skilled at being where no one expects him and gone before anyone notices he was there. He uses this generously on the group's behalf, and occasionally on the group's gold. The winged boots were purchased from Old Man Jenkins using the party's shared treasury. His explanation of this as a "slick deal" was, by general consensus, insufficient. The boots work, which is the important part.

In Kalaman, during the council meeting, he spotted a member whose appearance shimmered faintly with illusion — a Dragon's Eye operative embedded in the city's government. They acknowledged each other without words. He has not shared this with the group. Later, a note appeared in Thieves' Cant: We need to talk.
