The Red Dragon Army

The dragon army forces known to the party. Organized, disciplined, and marching on Ansalon.

Threat Level: Critical
Overview

The Red Dragon Army is a organized military force serving Takhisis, the Shadow Queen. They are disciplined, well-equipped, and composed of both humanoid soldiers and draconians — reptilian creatures with scales, wings, and draconic features.

The party first encountered them on the road to Vogler, where they attacked a caravan of refugees. The creatures were led by humanoid officers and spoke in Draconic, coordinating their assault with military precision.

First Encounter

Location: Road to Vogler

Context: The party was traveling when a caravan was ambushed by Dragon Army outriders. A knight fell with an arrow in his throat before combat began.

Outcome: The party defeated the initial force and captured one draconian alive for questioning.

What We Know
  • They operate in organized units with clear command structure
  • Officers speak Draconic and command the draconian troops in that language
  • They are well-armed with military-grade weapons and armor
  • The east has already fallen to their advance
  • They are moving west, pursuing civilian refugees and military forces
  • Their soldiers show no hesitation or mercy in combat
  • Named officers include Theros Flametongue (slain at Vogler) and Bakaris the Younger
Soldiers

The Red Dragon Army employs both humanoid soldiers (humans, other races) and draconians. The draconians are disciplined warriors with reptilian features and draconic heritage. They are formidable in combat and follow orders with chilling obedience.

Strategic Movement

The party encountered the Dragon Army attempting to catch refugee columns fleeing toward Kalaman. They have been pursuing displaced civilians, scouts, and military detachments across Ansalon. Their presence has forced the evacuation of towns and the abandonment of strategic positions.

Key Officers Known

Theros Flametongue — Dragon Army commander at Vogler. Encountered at the docks. Status: Slain (later resurrected).

Bakaris the Younger — Dragon Army officer. Encountered at High Hill during the refugee crisis.

The Threat

The Red Dragon Army is not a scattered band of raiders. It is a conquering force with the backing of a dragon queen and the organization of a kingdom. Their presence signals that the conflict is far larger than local skirmishes — it is a war for the fate of Ansalon itself.
