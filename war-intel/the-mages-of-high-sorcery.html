The Mages of High Sorcery

Keepers of arcane knowledge and practice

Faction: Neutral
Faction: Neutral
The Order

The Mages of High Sorcery are an ancient and exclusive order of spellcasters. They are bound together by their mastery of magic and their loyalty to the principles of the craft. The order is not political — they do not align with kingdoms or nations, but rather with the pursuit of magical knowledge and the balance of arcane power in the world.

The Three Robes

The Mages of High Sorcery are organized into three robes, distinguished by the color of their garments and the nature of their magical practice. Each robe represents a different alignment and philosophy within the order.

  • Mages of different robes often pursue different goals and priorities
  • The robes are bound together by common purpose and shared training
  • Advancement within the order requires magical prowess and demonstrated mastery
Entry & Trial

Admission to the Mages of High Sorcery is competitive and dangerous. Kakashi and Jasper participated in a trial within a mysterious arcane chamber. The trial tested their intelligence, caution, and magical ability. Those who pass are admitted to the order and granted access to the secrets of the craft.

Mystery & Isolation

The Mages of High Sorcery are notoriously secretive. They maintain sanctuaries hidden from the mundane world. One such sanctuary is the Tower of Wayreth — a place of immense magical power that moves through time and space, accessible only to those the Mages deem worthy. Knowledge of these places and their purposes is carefully guarded.

Current Situation

How the Mages of High Sorcery respond to the Shadow Queen's invasion is unclear. The order values neutrality and the pursuit of knowledge above political allegiance. However, the existence of a force powerful enough to threaten the entire continent may force even the isolated Mages to take a stance.
