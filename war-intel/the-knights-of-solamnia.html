The Order

The Knights of Solamnia are one of the greatest knighthoods in Ansalon. They are bound by oath, by honor, and by the Measure — a code that governs their actions and ideals. They are warriors, but more than that — they are protectors, sworn to defend the innocent and uphold the balance against forces of darkness.

Reputation

The Knights of Solamnia have a complicated legacy in the hearts of the people of Ansalon. Many believe the Knights failed to protect their people during the Cataclysm — a calamity that shattered the world ages ago. That failure has cast a shadow on their honor that persists to this day.

However, in recent years, their reputation has begun to recover. Their dedication to the ideals of protection and justice has been proven time and again. The order is finding its way back to the esteem it once held.

Why They Matter Now

Aratos Stormblessed is determined to reach Kalaman and warn the Knights of Solamnia about the coming of the Shadow Queen. If anyone has the power and organization to stand against Takhisis's armies, it is the Knights. They may be the only force capable of mounting an effective resistance.

Known Members
  • Becklin Uth Viharin — A Knight of the Crown. Encountered in Vogler and died defending refugees.
Structure & Oaths

The Knights of Solamnia are organized into distinct orders, each bound by sacred oaths. The exact nature of these orders is not fully known to the party, but the discipline and honor the Knights display suggests a deep spiritual and martial tradition.

Current Situation

The Knights are not yet unified in their response to the Dragon Queen's invasion. Some fortifications have fallen. Some Knights have made the ultimate sacrifice. But the order is mobilizing, and their strength is being gathered to meet the threat.

Whether the Knights of Solamnia can rise to meet this challenge — and whether they will answer the call of one desperate barbarian bearing visions of darkness — may determine the fate of Ansalon itself.
