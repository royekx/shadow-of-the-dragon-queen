Takhisis, the Shadow Queen

The Shadow Queen — The power driving the Dragon Armies

Critical Threat
Threat Level: Apocalyptic
The Name

Takhisis is the name spoken by the draconians and soldiers of the Red Dragon Army. She is called the Shadow Queen. Her influence spreads across Ansalon, her armies march in her name, and her will drives the conquest that threatens to swallow the continent.

What We Know
  • She commands the Red Dragon Army and the draconians that serve her
  • The draconians speak of her with reverence and fear
  • She is described as a power of shadow and darkness
  • Her coming — or her return — has destabilized Ansalon
  • She is not merely a military commander but something far greater
  • Her influence touches the very land, bringing change and ruin
First Knowledge

During the ambush on the road to Vogler, the captured draconian named her when questioned. It spoke of reclaiming the realm for the Shadow Queen, said the east had already fallen, and warned that the shadow was coming fast. Then it choked itself to death rather than reveal more.

The Vision

Aratos experienced a vision before any of this began — a dream of scaled warriors marching in ordered ranks, their weapons drawn and their purpose certain. A command settled in his bones: fight. The vision was terrifying and urgent, pointing him toward Kalaman and the Knights of Solamnia.

Mayriel witnessed divine fire and draconic terror in a vision near an old holy place. Something ancient spoke, and it was not gentle.

The Threat

Takhisis is not a distant enemy. Her armies are moving across the land. Her draconians are disciplined and ruthless. Her will is focused on conquest. The fact that she is willing to destroy entire towns, chase refugees, and rebuild the world in her image suggests that her ambitions are total and absolute.

Strategic Reality

If the east has already fallen, and her armies are pushing west with this discipline and power, then Ansalon faces not a regional conflict but an existential crisis. The Knights of Solamnia, the Mages of High Sorcery, and every force capable of resistance will be needed to stand against her.
