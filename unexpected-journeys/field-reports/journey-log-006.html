The Dragon Queen's Demand

An ultimatum in red and gold, delivered at sunset. Surrender or burn.

The Council
  • Mayor Raven looks exhausted. The weight of every villager sits on her shoulders.
  • Becklin and Darrett study a map, marking reinforcement points.
  • Kakashi's raven returns with news from Cudgel: she found her camp, some mercenaries were captured, and she is returning soon.
  • Cudgel arrives with Jeyev, one of her loyal lieutenants.
The Ultimatum
  • Cudgel confirms the worst: the army is real, positioned to the northwest. Some loyal mercenaries survived the betrayal.
  • She lays out the hard truth: Vogler can stay and fight, or it can evacuate.
  • Mayor Raven resists. Vogler is not merely buildings and docks. It is home.
  • The party presses the need for a plan beyond pride. Even if it is a last resort.
Preparation
  • Becklin quietly agrees that escape must be prepared. The river and boats offer a way out, though repairs will take time.
  • Cudgel offers to send word to her mercenaries to help hold the northern approach.
  • Kakashi considers sending his raven toward Kalaman, though that will take time they do not have.
  • As sunset approaches, an armored messenger arrives with demands written in red and gold: surrender by nightfall, or burn.
The Choice
  • There is no time left. The choice crystallizes: defend to the last, or flee.
  • Becklin makes clear what the party already knows — staying means death. Not victory. Death.
  • The evacuation begins. Boats are prepared. The people of Vogler gather their most precious things.
  • Night falls. The Red Army waits. The choice has been made, and it will be paid for in blood.
