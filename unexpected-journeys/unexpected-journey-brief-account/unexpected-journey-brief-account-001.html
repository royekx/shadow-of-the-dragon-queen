Five Strangers
  • Aratos Stormblessed — barbarian carrying a vision of Takhisis and lightning in his bones, compelled to find the Knights of Solamnia
  • Kakashi — wizard with a raven familiar and questions about the moons and magic's place in the world
  • Jasper Quickwit — rogue with quick hands and quicker wit, carrying secrets about the Dragon's Eye
  • Roshi — paladin of Kiri-Jolith, devoted to honor and to protecting the innocent
  • Mayriel — cleric of Paladine, walking the roads to understand what the gods are saying to her
The Ambush
  • Draconian creatures attack without warning — reptilian humanoids with scaled hides, claws, and draconic speech
  • Jasper strikes from hiding. Kakashi unleashes lightning. Roshi grapples and demands answers. Mayriel understands the enemy's words naming the Shadow Queen. Aratos charges with the force of an omen still burning in him.
  • One creature is captured alive. When pressed for answers, it speaks of reclaiming the realm for its queen and warns that the east has already fallen
  • Rather than be interrogated further, the prisoner chooses death — chokes itself to death, refusing to reveal more
What They Learn
  • This is the party's first encounter with creatures of organized, draconic forces. The Dragon Armies are real.
  • The prisoner's final act — choosing death over speech — hints at something vast and terrible moving across Ansalon
  • Roshi and Mayriel both experience strange moments: Roshi near an old holy site hears whispers of the gods. Mayriel experiences a vision of divine significance.
  • The road to Vogler now feels like a road toward something larger than any of them understood
