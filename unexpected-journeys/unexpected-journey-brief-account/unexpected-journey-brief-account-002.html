The Nightmare
  • Jasper's dream — a voice whispers, offering power. A coin sits in his palm, alive with purpose. When he wakes, the coin is still there.
  • Kakashi experiences a vision of moons aligned in patterns he doesn't understand, as if something is tuning the world.
  • Something in the earth moves beneath them. Jasper nearly disappears into a tunnel of teeth and tentacles — a roper, a creature as old as stone.
Fizban Emerges
  • The party fights the creature. Jasper is nearly swallowed; the others tear it apart.
  • Fizban crawls from the creature's gullet — an old wizard in green robes, muttering about poor accommodations and worse company.
  • Fizban claims to be an old friend of Ispin Greenshield, a legendary adventurer. The party has never heard of him.
  • He asks few questions and offers even fewer answers, but he joins them without hesitation.
The Road to Vogler
  • Fizban tells stories of Ispin's impossible life — goblins, owlbears, narrow escapes, laughter in the face of death.
  • The river carries rumors from the town ahead: the fish are vanishing. Something is wrong in the water.
  • Strange sounds have been heard from beneath Vogler. No one speaks of it directly, but fear lives in the gaps between words.
  • The town waits ahead. Ispin Greenshield's funeral will be tonight.
