Vogler Welcomes
  • Becklin Uth Viharin — a retired Knight of Solamnia — knew Ispin well. She hears Aratos's warning about the visions and Dragon Armies with the respect of someone grounded enough to carry fear forward.
  • Mayriel experiences a vision near a fish statue at the docks: a battlefield, a horned woman commanding slaughter, a five-headed dragon, and platinum fire rising in answer.
  • Aeimos Stormblessed appears — Aratos's cousin, with the same storm-marked blood. He steals Aratos's ring as a prank before revealing himself. Their reunion carries warmth and the roughness of shared blood.
The Island
  • Strange sounds from the river. Fishermen report poor catches. Kakashi's raven scouts an abandoned shack on Crustdurn Isle.
  • The party rents a rowboat. The island is rumored to be haunted, avoided by locals, marked by a silence that makes curiosity feel like trespass.
  • Below the shack: a cavern. Fish bones litter the ground. Old writings suggest something beneath needed to be fed or appeased.
  • The creature wakes — a grotesque thing of frog-like mass and grasping tentacles.
The Froghemoth
  • Kakashi strikes first with ice. Jasper disappears into shadows. Aratos and Aeimos bring steel and lightning. Mayriel channels divine harm. Roshi charges into danger.
  • The creature swallows Roshi. The battle becomes frantic — save Roshi before the creature can digest her.
  • Aratos drives lightning through its body. Jasper's arrows find killing angles. Aeimos leaps into the fray with storm-charged fists. Together, the Stormblast cousins and their companions bring it down.
  • Aeimos cuts out the creature's tongue — a grim trophy.
Treasures
  • Within the cavern: treasures from things dragged or offered to the beast over years.
  • A moon-touched sword and boots marked by strange magic.
  • The town of Vogler, for one more day at least, is safe.
