Tales, Tears, and Treachery

Ispin's memory became a shield, and Vogler became a battlefield.

The Funeral
  • At sunset, Ispin's body is placed in a small boat on the Vingaard River. Becklin and Cudgel lower it. The people watch until it drifts from sight.
  • Inside the Brass Crab, grief becomes laughter. Tankards clash. Ispin stories rise from every corner.
  • Fizban tells of a stolen pie and an offended prince. Roshi tells of Ispin pursuing honey in a bear den, waiting for the bears to wake so he could compliment them.
  • Kakashi speaks quietly: Ispin was a man guided by curiosity rather than fear. The room goes still. Then raised their drinks.
The Festival
  • The Kingfisher Festival's centerpiece: a mock battle reenacting Vogler's founding. Mayor Raven leads the parade. The party takes part. It is pageantry — until an unpadded blade opens a real wound.
  • The mock battle becomes a massacre. Cudgel's mercenaries — paid to betray — turn their weapons on the militia.
  • Jasper kills a traitor. Darrett Highwater falls. Aratos holds the line. Roshi stands with Ispin's shield. Mayriel's divine light burns wild and devastating. Kakashi presses magic from the air.
The Betrayal
  • A half-ogre drives Roshi near death. She answers with divine fire. Her blade erupts with searing power. Jasper puts an arrow through the ogre's head as it falls.
  • Becklin and Cudgel arrive and push the remainder into retreat.
  • On the half-ogre: a note. "For your services from the Red Army."
  • An organized force. Not raiders. An army. And it has already paid for betrayal inside Vogler.
