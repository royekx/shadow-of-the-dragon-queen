Unexpected Journey 009

The Reliquary of Lost Oaths

Beneath a hidden door, knights make their stand against corruption itself.

The Hunt
  • The party sets out to hunt for Fizban's stew. The wilds beyond Kalaman are quiet, watchful.
  • They find signs of an old conflict — scarred earth, weathered stone, the sense of something sacred that was abandoned long ago.
  • Beneath a hidden door, a reliquary. The resting place of Knights Radiant — old guardians, sworn to oaths that transcended death.
  • The party enters.
The Oaths
  • The reliquary tests them. Knights Radiant oaths demand honesty, courage, and purpose from those who would disturb the dead.
  • Aratos and Aeimos feel the pull of something older than they are — a recognition, a kinship with the oath-keepers who sleep here.
  • Kakashi, Jasper, Roshi, and Mayriel stand steadfast. They are not knights of the old order, but their purpose is clear.
  • The oaths are satisfied. A door opens deeper still.
The Corruption
  • In the deepest chamber: Sir Cthondor, a wraith knight — bound by oath to guard the reliquary even beyond death.
  • But he has been corrupted. A necromancer has twisted his spirit, turned his oath inward, trapped him in eternal torment.
  • A pale woman stands beside Cthondor — a guide or a guard, her purpose unclear. Draconians move in the shadows.
  • The party fights. They break the corruption. They free Sir Cthondor from the chains that bound him.
The Recognition
  • Sir Cthondor, freed and himself again, looks upon Aratos and Aeimos with the weight of ancient knowledge.
  • "You carry the mark of Cathan. You are his children, whether you knew it or not."
  • Before his spirit departs to final rest, he speaks a truth the party did not expect: they are Knights Radiant — not by ordination, but by the oaths they carry in their hearts.
  • The reliquary seals behind them. The hunt for stew ingredients is over. The hunt for answers has only begun.
