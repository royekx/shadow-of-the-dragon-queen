Unexpected Journey 007

Flames Over Vogler

The sky darkens with wings. A dragon comes to Krynn.

UJ 007 Brief Account → Full Account
A mechanical dragon appears over Vogler's docks, breathing fire. The party destroys the construct, but Becklin falls into the river. The town burns.

The Sky Darkens
  • The last boats of Vogler strain against the docks. Smoke rolls across the river.
  • The evacuation has nearly succeeded when the sky darkens with wings. Someone looks up and cries the word not spoken in Krynn for centuries: dragon.
  • The creature sweeps over the docks with its mouth full of flame. Below, the last defenders clash with draconians, buying seconds for the villagers to escape.
  • Then the dragon breathes. Fire pours across the ground. Those still in its path are reduced to ash.
Not Quite Dragon
  • The creature is not alive. It is mechanical — gears and steam and copper wings, driven by something that wears the shape of a dragon.
  • Theros Flametongue — an orc war captain — commands the construct and the coordinated assault.
  • Than Rookledust, a gnome tinkerer, launches himself from Thornwall Keep on a self-built contraption. He lands hard and sees: the dragon is not alone. Smaller winged shapes move through the smoke.
  • And there is something riding the great beast, something that leaps down toward the battlefield.
The Fight
  • The party engages. Aratos brings lightning. Kakashi presses ice magic. Jasper finds angles in the chaos. Roshi stands solid. Mayriel's divine wrath burns.
  • Becklin fights alongside them, holding the line with the discipline of a knight who has faced worse and lived.
  • The mechanical dragon is damaged. Gears jam. Steam leaks. The party tears it apart — but at terrible cost.
  • Becklin falls. She tumbles into the Vingaard River, the current carrying her downstream faster than any of them can reach.
Ashes
  • The dragon falls. Theros and his draconians withdraw.
  • Behind them, Vogler burns. The town that welcomed them in Ispin's name is consumed by fire, steel, and the proof that dragons walk Krynn again.
  • The refugees float downriver, carrying what they could save and the knowledge that nothing will ever be the same.
  • A Knight of Solamnia is gone, swept away in the river named after the god of war himself.
