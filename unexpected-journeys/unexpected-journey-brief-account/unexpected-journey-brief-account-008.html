The River of Ghosts
  • The Vingaard River carries the survivors away from Vogler, but cannot carry them away from what they have seen.
  • Smoke swallows the town that welcomed them. Ahead waits Kalaman, half a day downriver. Between those two places, the boats drift through grief.
  • Roshi finds Darrett Highwater shaken by the weight of all that happened. Becklin is gone. Vogler is burning.
  • Roshi presents him with Becklin's armor. Darrett cannot bring himself to accept it at first. He did not do enough. He did not save Vogler.
Kalaman Rising
  • The refugees reach Kalaman. Governor Miat and Marshal Vendri meet them at the gates.
  • Mayor Raven petitions for aid. Kalaman is willing to shelter the people of Vogler — but there is a price.
  • The council wants the party to serve in the city's defense. An agreement is made. Magical tests are scheduled.
  • The people of Vogler are settled as best they can be — tents, fresh water, food rations. Most are grateful. A few are bitter.
The Offer
  • Fizban, ever strange and seemingly untouched by ordinary urgency, suggests a remedy for despair: stew.
  • He claims to know one of Ispin's old recipes — a hearty dish that might fill empty stomachs and lift wounded spirits.
  • He needs meat. Bear, deer, hare, anything the wilds might offer.
  • The party, needing something simple after days of fire and grief, agrees to hunt.
The Gift
  • Kakashi attunes himself to the Boilerdrake Heart, the hot gem taken from the dragon's wreckage.
  • Aratos takes up the Emberwake Greataxe, its heat and promise waiting beneath the surface.
  • The river keeps moving. For a little while, exhaustion gives the survivors silence.
  • And somewhere in the night, a messenger arrives with news: Lord Soth walks the lands.
