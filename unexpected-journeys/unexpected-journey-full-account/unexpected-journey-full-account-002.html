Unexpected Journey 002

The Road After Bloodshed

On the road to Vogler, nightmares grow teeth and sand turns predator.

← Previous Brief Account Next →
UJ 002 Party Level 1 Vingaard River

After the ambush, the road grew quieter, but not safer. The dead knights carried signs that they had been investigating attacks in the region, and the party took what clues they could before giving the fallen their rest. Roshi treated the dead with solemn care, while the others gathered weapons, supplies, and whatever might help them survive what waited ahead. The bodies of the dragon-like attackers decayed with disturbing speed, and the party chose fire rather than leaving them to rot beside the road.

Night brought no true peace. Jasper's sleep returned him to the shadow of the Dragon's Eye, the guild that had marked him and bound him with a deadly coin. The memory of Lucky, the guildmaster, pressed on him like a debt not yet paid. Jasper had been given an impossible task: infiltrate the world of mages, find the moving Tower of Wayreth, and steal the Orb of Dragonkind. The words of the guild clung to him like a curse: none shall hide from the Dragon's Eye.

Kakashi's watch brought its own omen. Under the moons, he saw them align in a way that felt deliberate and wrong. Voices whispered that he was not strong enough, not yet, and images flooded his mind of archmages shaping fire, ice, beasts, and time itself. His eyes flared with power, and the hunger for greater magic stirred in him with new urgency.

The next day, the road itself seemed to betray them. The party discovered a man half-swallowed by the earth, one foot sticking from the ground as if the world had tried to eat him. Jasper approached with caution, only to be caught in the same strange pull. As the party fought to free him, a living mass of sand rose around them, and from within it came the voice of a very old man treating his predicament with baffling cheer.

The battle against the sand creature was desperate and strange. Roshi was struck hard and nearly killed. Mayriel rushed to preserve her life. Kakashi's magic thundered through the air. Jasper struck at the frozen creature when openings appeared. Aratos brought lightning against the thing, and the old man within finally unleashed magic of his own, freezing the creature solid before the party shattered it. When the danger passed, the old man introduced himself as Fizban, a peculiar wizard in green robes and another old friend of Ispin.

Fizban joined them on the road to Vogler. He took a particular interest in Mayriel's divine magic, noting that clerics had not walked openly in centuries and that if the gods were speaking again, something serious was unfolding. To Kakashi, he offered indirect but meaningful counsel about wizardry and the paths that might await him. His presence was comic, strange, and unsettlingly insightful all at once.

At last, the party reached Vogler. The village stood beside the Vingaard River, alive with preparations for the Kingfisher Festival and Ispin's funeral. Roshi sought food and memory. Jasper explored and noticed an unusual figure near Thornwall Keep. Mayriel walked the docks. The town itself seemed warm and full of ordinary life, but beneath the festival cheer, the party began hearing the same troubling rumor from different mouths: the fish were vanishing, and strange sounds had been heard from the river.
