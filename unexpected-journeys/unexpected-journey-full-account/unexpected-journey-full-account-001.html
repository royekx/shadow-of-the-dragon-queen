Unexpected Journey 001

The Night of the Eye

Beneath the watchful light of Krynn's three moons, a vision becomes a command.

UJ 001 Party Level 1 Road to Vogler

Beneath the watchful light of Krynn's three moons, Aratos woke from a dream that felt more like a summons than sleep. In the vision, scaled warriors marched across the land in ordered ranks, their bodies shaped like men but marked by the savagery of dragons. Their weapons were drawn, their purpose certain, and their coming filled the air with the promise of ruin. Somewhere within the terror of it, Aratos felt a command settle into his bones: fight. When he woke, the dream did not fade. It lingered like smoke in his lungs, pointing him toward Kalaman and the Knights of Solamnia.

Elsewhere, Kakashi and Jasper stood among hopeful mages preparing for a trial that would decide whether they were worthy to continue their studies. Kakashi faced the test with discipline and focus, while Jasper carried a quieter purpose beneath his apprentice's mask. The trial placed them in a strange chamber of invisible passages, hidden writing, and a locked door whose obvious key seemed almost too inviting to trust. Jasper studied the runes and uncovered the word needed to open the way, while Kakashi saw through the false promise of the key itself. Together, through caution and cleverness, they passed the test and were rewarded with a moon-marked spellbook, along with a path that pointed them onward.

Roshi and Mayriel traveled under a different banner: grief. Letters had drawn them toward Vogler for the funeral of Ispin Greenshield, an old adventurer whose brief human life had somehow left lasting marks upon elven memory. On the road, they followed strange sounds into the dark and found themselves near the remnants of an old holy place. There, Mayriel saw a vision of divine fire and draconic terror, while Roshi felt the weight of a sacred calling settle over her like a hand upon the shoulder. The memory blurred afterward, but the feeling did not. Something ancient had spoken, and it had not spoken idly.

Their separate roads met in violence before any of them reached Vogler. A caravan was ambushed by reptilian humanoids with scaled hides, sharp claws, brutal weapons, and voices that spoke in Draconic. One knight fell with an arrow in his throat, and in the space of a breath, strangers became allies. Jasper vanished into the terrain and struck from hiding. Kakashi lashed out with lightning. Roshi threw herself bodily into danger, grappling one of the creatures and demanding answers. Mayriel understood the enemy's words when it named the Shadow Queen. Aratos recognized the soldiers from his dream and charged into battle with the force of the omen still burning in him.

When the last creature was captured, the group pressed it for answers. It spoke of reclaiming the realm for its queen, said the east had already fallen, and warned that the shadow was coming fast. Then, rather than reveal more, it choked itself to death. The strangers were left among dead knights, unnatural corpses, and the first shared knowledge that their separate journeys had drawn them into the same darkness.

They buried the fallen as best they could, burned the bodies of the scaled attackers before their unnatural decay could spread, and gathered what supplies remained. Vogler and Ispin's funeral still lay ahead, but the road no longer felt like a simple passage from one place to another. Something had begun.
